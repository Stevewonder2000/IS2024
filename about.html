About Me

A little background.

My love of second-hand shopping springs from growing up in sunny Redlands, CA and getting to yard sale amist the orange groves year-around with my mom. I have such happy memories of scoring the best deals with her during those times. She really taught me that fashion on a budget is more than just possible...it's more fun. Yard saling and going to thrift stores with her was'nt about getting exactly what I wanted for my waredrobe when I wanted it. It was more of a fun game to see if I could find the cutest thing at the lowest price.

As I've gotten older my yardsaling adventures have turned in a dream of someday opening up my own second hand boutique. Right now, I'm still focusing hard on school so that dream is gonna have to wait a bit. In the mean time, I take pride in being the thrift expert wherever I'm stationed at :)
